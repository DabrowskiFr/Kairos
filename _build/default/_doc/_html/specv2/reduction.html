Clauses And Triples

The reduction is expressed in two layers.

  • Generated Clauses
  • Relational Triples

Generated Clauses

SpecV2.GeneratedClauses defines semantic clauses over one tick context:

  • initialization clauses;
  • propagation clauses;
  • safety clauses excluding dangerous steps.

Relational Triples

SpecV2.RelationalTriples packages the generated clauses into local proof obligations. Triples are either attached to the initial tick or to one transition label.
