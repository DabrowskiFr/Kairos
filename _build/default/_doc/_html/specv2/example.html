Resettable Delay Example

SpecV2.ResettableDelayExample is the concrete client of the abstract theory. It defines:

  • the reactive program resettable_delay_program;
  • the assumption and guarantee automata;
  • the user invariant used by the generated triples;
  • the theorem resettable_delay_correct.

The final result is obtained through the abstract soundness theorem rather than by a direct ad hoc proof on traces.
