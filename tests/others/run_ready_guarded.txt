Reason:
- The specification appears consistent with the program:
  - `go = 1` enters `Run` and keeps `mode = 1`
  - in `Run`, `ready` follows `err`
- The remaining failing VCs are on the `Idle -> Idle` step.
- The exported product currently keeps a safe branch for `go = 0` whose
  guarantee side still talks about `mode = 1` / `ready`, even though the
  program transition `Idle -> Idle` sets `mode := 0` and `ready := 0`.

Diagnosis:
- This points to a product-construction issue.
- The current product builder keeps some combinations based on overlap between
  guards, but does not reject guarantee branches that are incompatible with the
  actual effect of the program transition.

Current status:
- This is not treated as a test-spec issue.
- It should stay out of automated campaigns until the product construction is
  fixed for this case.
